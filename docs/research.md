What is ERRANT?

ERRANT (ERRor ANnotation Toolkit) wasd developed by academic researcher Christopher Bryant, Mariano Felice and Ted Briscoe. It is rreferenced on the University of Cambridge Language Sciences portal at https://www.languagesciences.cam.ac.uk/resources. ERRANT analyses students' uncorrected senetences, compares them against corrected versions, and generates error codes identifying the students' mistakes.
